## ❓ **FAQ:**

### **Q: Do I need netlify.toml?**
**A:** NO! You can ignore it. Just use the Netlify website form.
But netlify.toml is there if you want advanced configuration later.

### **Q: Will my site update automatically when I push code?**
**A:** YES! Netlify watches your GitHub. Every push auto-deploys.

### **Q: What about my backend server?**
**A:** Deploy separately (Heroku, Railway, Render, or your own server).
Frontend and backend are separate deployments.

### **Q: How much does Netlify cost?**
**A:** FREE for basic hosting! Premium plans available later.

### **Q: How do I use a custom domain?**
**A:** After deployment: Site settings → Domain management
Add your domain (bluefins.com, etc.)

## 📋 **WHAT THE NETLIFY DEPLOYMENT PROCESS DOES:**

When you click "Deploy site":

```
1. Netlify clones your GitHub repo
   ↓
2. Navigates to client folder (Base directory: client)
   ↓
3. Installs dependencies (npm install)
   ↓
4. Runs build (npm run build)
   ↓
5. Creates optimized dist/ folder
   ↓
6. Uploads to Netlify CDN
   ↓
7. Assigns URL: https://your-site-name.netlify.app
   ↓
8. Enables HTTPS automatically
   ↓
9. Your site is LIVE! 🎉
```

All automatic! You just click deploy.
